## Lecture Overview

BEARS is excited to welcome Dr. James Okoro, a systems engineer at Planet Labs, for a guest lecture on small satellite design and operations. Planet Labs operates the largest constellation of Earth-imaging satellites, and Dr. Okoro will share the engineering story behind it.

### Topics

- **CubeSat Design Constraints**: Working within strict mass, volume, and power budgets
- **Constellation Operations**: Managing hundreds of satellites in low Earth orbit
- **Earth Observation**: How satellite imagery is captured, processed, and delivered
- **Lessons Learned**: What goes wrong in space and how to design for resilience

### About the Speaker

Dr. James Okoro has spent eight years at Planet Labs working on spacecraft avionics and mission operations. He holds a PhD in Aerospace Engineering and previously worked at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center.
