## Event Overview

Time for our mid-semester progress review! Each active project team will present a short update on their progress, challenges, and next steps. Afterwards, stick around for pizza and board games.

### Agenda

- **5:30 PM** - Doors open, snacks
- **6:00 PM** - Project updates (5 min each)
  - Rocket Propulsion Team
  - Autonomous Drone Squad
  - CubeSat Development Group
  - Rover Navigation Team
- **7:00 PM** - Q&A and open discussion
- **7:30 PM** - Pizza, drinks, and social games

### Why Attend?

- See what every team has been working on
- Find a project to join if you haven't already
- Meet members from other teams
- Give feedback and share ideas across projects
