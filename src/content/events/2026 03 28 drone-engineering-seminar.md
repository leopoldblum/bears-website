## Seminar Overview

An evening seminar featuring talks from engineers working on cutting-edge drone technology. Learn about the challenges and breakthroughs in modern UAV design from people building the real thing.

### Speakers

- **Tom Rivera** (Wing Aviation) - Last-mile delivery drone logistics
- **Dr. Priya Sharma** (Skydio) - Visual navigation and obstacle avoidance
- **Marcus Lee** (BEARS Alumni, DJI) - From student projects to industry careers

### Topics Covered

- **Flight Controller Design**: PID tuning, sensor fusion, and real-time control systems
- **Autonomous Navigation**: SLAM, computer vision, and GPS-denied flight
- **Regulations & Safety**: FAA Part 107, beyond visual line of sight operations
- **Career Paths**: How to break into the drone industry as an engineer
