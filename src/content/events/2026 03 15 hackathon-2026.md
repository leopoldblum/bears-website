## BEARS 24-Hour Hackathon

Get ready for the most exciting event of the semester! Our annual hackathon challenges teams to design, build, and program innovative solutions within 24 hours.

### Theme: Autonomous Systems for Sustainability

This year's hackathon focuses on creating autonomous systems that address environmental challenges. Whether it's waste sorting robots, energy-efficient drones, or smart agricultural systems, we want to see your creative solutions.

### Competition Details

- **Duration**: 24 hours non-stop
- **Team Size**: 2-4 members
- **Prizes**:
  - 1st Place: $1,000 + Lab Access for a semester
  - 2nd Place: $500 + BEARS Swag Pack
  - 3rd Place: $250 + Project Mentorship

### What's Provided

- Development workstations and tools
- Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and sensor kits
- Meals and snacks throughout the event
- Mentorship from industry professionals
- 3D printing and fabrication access

### Schedule

**Day 1**
- 9:00 AM - Registration & Team Formation
- 10:00 AM - Challenge Announcement & Kickoff
- 12:00 PM - Lunch
- All afternoon/evening - Hacking!

**Day 2**
- 9:00 AM - Final Push
- 10:00 AM - Submissions Due
- 11:00 AM - Project Demonstrations
- 2:00 PM - Awards Ceremony
