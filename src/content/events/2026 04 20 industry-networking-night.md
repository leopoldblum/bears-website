## Event Overview

Join us for an evening of professional networking with leaders from the aerospace and engineering industries. This is your opportunity to make meaningful connections, learn about career paths, and discover internship and job opportunities at top companies.

### Featured Companies

We're excited to welcome representatives from:
- **Boeing** - Commercial Airplanes & Defense Systems
- **SpaceX** - Launch & Spacecraft Engineering
- **Lockheed Martin** - Aeronautics & Space
- **Blue Origin** - New Glenn Program
- **Northrop Grumman** - Aerospace Systems
- **NASA JPL** - Jet Propulsion Laboratory
- Local startups and research organizations

### What's Included

**Networking Sessions**
- Informal meet-and-greet with industry professionals
- Small group discussions on career development
- One-on-one mentorship opportunities
- Alumni panel sharing their career journeys

**Resume & Portfolio Reviews**
- Bring your resume for professional feedback
- Tips on showcasing technical projects
- Guidance on applying to aerospace positions
- Interview preparation advice

**Company Presentations**
- Learn about ongoing projects and initiatives
- Understand company culture and values
- Discover internship programs and entry-level positions
- Q&A sessions with engineers and recruiters

**Career Development**
- Workshop on transitioning from student to professional
- Advice on building a strong engineering portfolio
- Insights into different career paths in aerospace
- Discussion on graduate school vs industry

### Event Schedule

- **6:00 PM** - Registration & Welcome Reception
- **6:30 PM** - Opening Remarks & Industry Overview
- **7:00 PM** - Company Spotlight Presentations (5 min each)
- **7:45 PM** - Networking Break & Refreshments
- **8:15 PM** - Breakout Sessions (Choose your track)
  - Career Path Discussions
  - Resume Review Stations
  - Technical Q&A Panels
- **9:15 PM** - Alumni Panel Discussion
- **9:45 PM** - Closing Remarks & Continued Networking
- **10:00 PM** - Event Concludes

### Who Should Attend

This event is perfect for:
- Students seeking internships for summer
- Those exploring career options in aerospace/engineering
- Anyone interested in learning from industry professionals
- Students preparing for job applications
- Those looking to expand their professional network

All majors welcome! While focused on aerospace and engineering, we encourage students from related fields (computer science, physics, mathematics) to attend.

### What to Bring

- Multiple copies of your resume (at least 10)
- Portfolio or examples of your work (optional)
- Business cards if you have them
- Notebook for taking notes
- Questions for the professionals
- Professional attire recommended (business casual)

### Preparation Tips

To make the most of this event:
1. Research the attending companies beforehand
2. Prepare thoughtful questions about their work
3. Practice your elevator pitch (30-second introduction)
4. Update your LinkedIn profile
5. Review your resume for typos and formatting
6. Bring your enthusiasm and curiosity!
