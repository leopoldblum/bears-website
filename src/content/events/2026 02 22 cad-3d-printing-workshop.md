## Workshop Overview

Join us for a practical afternoon learning CAD design and 3D printing. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to sharpen your skills, this workshop has something for everyone.

### What You'll Learn

- **SolidWorks Basics**: Creating parts, assemblies, and engineering drawings
- **Design for 3D Printing**: Overhangs, supports, tolerances, and material considerations
- **Hands-On Printing**: Prepare and print your own design on our Prusa and Bambu Lab printers
- **Tips & Tricks**: Time-saving techniques from experienced BEARS members

### What to Bring

- Laptop with SolidWorks installed (student license available)
- A project idea or component you'd like to design
- USB drive for file transfer

**Date**: February 22, 2026
**Time**: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
