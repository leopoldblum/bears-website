title: "What is BEARS e.V.?"
description: "The Berlin Experimental Astronautics Research Student team e.V. (BEARS) is a student space team based at TU Berlin, Germany that continuously realises space related projects relevant for the European Space Sector independently, in cooperation with Space Agencies like ESA and DLR in workshops and for contests like the European Rover Challenge for Space Exploration and more. Besides being famous for building handy and clever rockets, BEARS conducts also experimental research, develops new technologies and software that enhances Space Exploration techniques. BEARS additionally engages in relevant events and fairs to network and exchange information. Members are mainly students from TU Berlin, FU Berlin and other young and clever space enthusiasts."
