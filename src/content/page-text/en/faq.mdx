---
title: Frequently Asked Questions
subtitle: Got Questions?
description: Everything you need to know about joining and participating in BEARS e.V.
faqs:
  - question: How can I join BEARS e.V.?
    answer: >-
      Just show up to our weekly general meetings for introduction purposes and
      to determine, which project would suit you best! We have a membership
      regulation that requires participation in our next meetings. Furthermore,
      you can become an active club member through a trial semester. 

      We welcome all students and space enthusiasts of Berlin/Potsdam
      Universities, regardless of their field of study, or experience level! To
      enter BEARS, we recommend joining one of our plenty project groups, but if
      you bring your own project, we are curious to evaluate, if it fits to our
      mission goals and values and might receive fundings. We most probably can
      arrange an introductory presentation slot for you to let active BEARS
      members make an educated vote. But our focus lays on existing projects
      that already have a stable foundation and designated plan. 

      See the "When/where to find us" section above for our meeting time and
      location.
  - question: Do I need prior aerospace experience?
    answer: >-
      Not at all! BEARS is a place for students to apply their theoretical
      knowledge gained in their studies to a real world project. Every student
      is welcome to join us - no matter the experience level. 
  - question: What is the time commitment?
    answer: >-
      Your work at BEARS will be two-fold: participation in one of our hardware
      projects as well as club-work, which could be involvement in events,
      trade-fairs, recruitment campaigns or social media activity.
      Time-commitment for projects is specific to the given project. For general
      club activity, we expect you to join our weekly meetings regularly (<1 h)
      and commit a few additional hours on a regular basis whenever there is
      work.
  - question: Are there membership fees?
    answer: >-
      BEARS e.V. charges a small annual membership fee to cover materials and
      operational costs. The exact amount is announced at the beginning of each
      semester. Financial constraints should never prevent you from joining —
      reach out to us if you have concerns.
  - question: Can I work on multiple projects?
    answer: >-
      Absolutely! Many of our members contribute to more than one project. It is
      a great way to broaden your skills and meet different team members.

      We also encourage a close interconnection of our individual project teams.
  - question: 'How can one become a BEARS sponsor with benefits? '
    answer: >-
      See our "Sponsors" page, or contact us directly to learn about our
      partnership opportunities!
