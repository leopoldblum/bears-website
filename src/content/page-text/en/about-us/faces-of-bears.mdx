description: >-
  Members participate in various project groups, which mainly are interlinked
  closely, but can be roughly divided in three main branches: the Rocketry/
  Propulsion, the Robotics/ Software and the Space exploration/Science.
  Furthermore, smaller teams that care about public relations, events, finances/
  sponsouring and solving club challenges are greatly appreciated through what
  also students with a broughter study background range can be part of BEARS.
  BEARS also has an excellent board and we are very proud of all of their
  efforts to keep BEARS outstanding and save our place in the space sector. The
  board mainly manages and strategizes key aspects for BEARS success, by
  including perspectives of all BEARS members through votings. 

  As our community is especially consisting out of students around the same age,
  our board is elected each term again, as some already finish their degrees and
  might move on towards other space sector projects. But BEARS stays connected
  with previous members, what adds additional value to our place in the space
  field and allows invitation to events and more.
