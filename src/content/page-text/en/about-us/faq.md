faqs:
  - question: "How can I join BEARS e.V.?"
    answer: "Just show up to one of our weekly meetings! We welcome all TU Berlin students, regardless of their field of study or experience level. Check the **Find Us** section above for our meeting time and location."
  - question: "Do I need prior aerospace experience?"
    answer: "Not at all! Our members come from diverse backgrounds — mechanical engineering, computer science, physics, and more. We provide training and mentoring so you can learn as you go."
  - question: "What is the time commitment?"
    answer: "That is entirely up to you. Most members attend our weekly meetings and contribute a few additional hours per week to their project teams. During competition season, some teams may meet more frequently."
  - question: "Are there membership fees?"
    answer: "BEARS e.V. charges a small annual membership fee to cover materials and operational costs. The exact amount is announced at the beginning of each semester. Financial constraints should never prevent you from joining — reach out to us if you have concerns."
  - question: "Can I work on multiple projects?"
    answer: "Absolutely! Many of our members contribute to more than one project. It is a great way to broaden your skills and meet different team members."
  - question: "How can my company sponsor BEARS?"
    answer: "We are always looking for sponsors to support our projects and events. Visit our [Sponsors page](/sponsors) or [get in touch](/contact) directly to discuss partnership opportunities."
