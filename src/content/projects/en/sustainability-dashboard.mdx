## Project Overview

The Campus Sustainability Dashboard is an innovative IoT project aimed at promoting environmental awareness and energy efficiency across university facilities. By collecting and visualizing real-time data on resource consumption, we empower the campus community to make informed decisions about sustainability.

### Mission

To create a transparent, data-driven platform that tracks environmental impact and encourages sustainable practices through awareness and actionable insights.

### System Components

**Hardware Layer**
- Smart energy meters for electricity monitoring
- Water flow sensors for consumption tracking
- Air quality sensors (CO2, particulate matter)
- Temperature and humidity sensors
- Smart waste bin sensors for fill levels

**Software Layer**
- Real-time data collection and processing pipeline
- Web-based dashboard with interactive visualizations
- Mobile app for on-the-go access
- Analytics engine for pattern detection
- Alerting system for anomalies

### Key Features

1. **Energy Monitoring**: Track electricity usage by building and department
2. **Water Usage Analytics**: Identify patterns and potential leaks
3. **Air Quality Tracking**: Monitor indoor environmental quality
4. **Waste Management**: Optimize collection routes based on bin fill levels
5. **Carbon Footprint Calculator**: Estimate campus-wide emissions
6. **Comparison Tools**: Benchmark performance against sustainability goals

### Technical Implementation

- **Backend**: Python with Flask for API services
- **Database**: InfluxDB for time-series data storage
- **Frontend**: Vue.js with D3.js for data visualization
- **IoT Platform**: MQTT for device communication
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: Self-hosted on university servers

### Impact & Results

Since deployment in pilot buildings:
- 15% reduction in energy waste through behavioral changes
- 20% improvement in waste collection efficiency
- Increased awareness of environmental impact among students and staff
- Data-driven insights for facility management decisions
