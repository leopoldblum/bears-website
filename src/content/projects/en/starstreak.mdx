STARSTREAK is our upcoming fifth rocket and the current focus of the Experimental Rocketry team. The rocket is a test platform for the redesign of all systems for the targeted launch of our sixth rocket at EuRoC27. These redesigns include a conical coupler design, a completely redesigned slottable modular Avionics system, and a reefing recovery system.
