Deutsche Übersetzung (vereinfacht):

STARSTREAK is our upcoming fifth Rakete and the current focus of the Experimental Raketery Team. The Rakete is a test platform for the redesign of all systems for the targeted launch of our sixth Rakete at EuRoC27. These redesigns include a conical coupler design, a completely redesigned slottable modular Avionics system, and a reefing recovery system.
