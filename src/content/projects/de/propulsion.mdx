Deutsche Übersetzung (vereinfacht):

Welcome to the Rakete Propulsion Team, where passionate Studierende work together to explore the exciting realm of Rakete engines. 
Our focus is on the Entwicklung of a bi-propellant pressure- fed propulsion system, with the goal of integrating it into our own Rakete, which is being developed by the Rakete System Team. 

## Current work
Currently, our efforts are centered on the construction of a test stand, a critical element for the testing and further Entwicklung of our engine.
The planning and procurement phase for the fluid system is complete. 
A specially designed swirl injector has been developed, and our propellant tanks are under construction. 
A small Team is busy with the Entwicklung and planning of the test stand electronics, ensuring that everything runs smoothly.
In collaboration with the Department of Fluid Dynamics, another segment of our Team is conducting water tests using the first iteration of our injector. 

## Next steps
This collaborative effort allows us to gather valuable insights and refine our design for optimal performance. 
Stay tuned for updates on the BEARS Instagram page, where we will share behind-the-scenes glimpses and exciting milestones.
Our timeline targets the completion of the test stand by the end of 2024. 
We understand that reaching for the stars requires meticulous planning, dedication, and collaboration, and we're thrilled to have you join us on this exciting journey.
