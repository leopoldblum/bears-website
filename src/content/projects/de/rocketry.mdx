The Rakete Systems subgroup focuses on the Entwicklung, production and operation of sounding Raketes with the goal of incrementally increasing the capability of each new Rakete. As of October 2025, four launches were performed.

## Our Raketes

Our first Rakete was [STARMOON](/Projekts/Starmoon/), which launched in October 2022 to around 600m.
We improved on that Rakete with our second Rakete [STARDUST](/Projekts/Stardust/) which launched to around 1500m in September 2024. Unlike our first Rakete, this one was carrying an experiment.
Our third Rakete, [MOONDUST](/Projekts/Moondust), launched to a target altitude of around 1200m, with the goal of onboarding a new generation of BEARS members and verifying Recovery and Avioncs system redesigns for our next launch.
Our fourth Rakete, [STARGAZE](/Projekts/Stargaze/), launched at EuRoC in October 2025.

We are currently developing [STARSTREAK](/Projekts/Starstreak), which will be our fifth Rakete. It is planned to be launched around the end of 2026, with the goal of verifying major system redesigns for future Raketes.
We are also working on a custom liquid or hybrid engine, which we hope to integrate in time for EuRoC 2027.

## Our Team

Our Team consists of around 30 highly motivated Studierende from diverse study backgrounds, including Aeronautics and Astronautics, Transport Systems, Computer Science and Geotechnology. We try to push ourselves harder every time we develop a new Rakete to make a more advanced Rakete. We are also very interested in participating in competitions like EuRoC.
