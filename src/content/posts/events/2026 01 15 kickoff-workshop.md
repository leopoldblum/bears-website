## Event Details

Welcome to BEARS' Spring 2026 Kickoff Workshop! This event marks the beginning of another exciting semester full of innovative projects and collaborative learning.

### What to Expect

- **Project Introductions**: Learn about the exciting projects we'll be working on this semester
- **Team Meet & Greet**: Connect with fellow members and find your project team
- **Technical Workshops**: Hands-on sessions covering tools and technologies we'll be using
- **Planning Session**: Help shape the direction of BEARS initiatives for the semester

### Schedule

- 5:00 PM - Registration & Welcome
- 5:30 PM - Project Presentations
- 6:30 PM - Break & Networking
- 7:00 PM - Technical Workshop Sessions
- 8:30 PM - Q&A and Planning Discussion

All students interested in aerospace, robotics, and engineering are welcome to attend!
