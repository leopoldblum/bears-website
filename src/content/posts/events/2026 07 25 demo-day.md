## Event Overview

BEARS concludes the academic year with our signature Demo Day - a celebration of innovation, hard work, and achievement! Join us for an afternoon of project demonstrations, research presentations, and recognition of our members' incredible accomplishments.

This is your opportunity to see what BEARS has been working on all year, from CubeSat tracking systems to autonomous rovers, sustainability dashboards to microgravity experiments. All are welcome!

**Live Demonstrations**
- Working prototypes and hardware systems
- Interactive displays you can try

- Real-time data visualizations
- Drone and robot demonstrations

**Project Presentations**

### Judging Criteria

Projects evaluated on:
- **Technical Merit** (30%) - Complexity, innovation, execution
- **Documentation** (20%) - Quality of written and visual materials
- **Presentation** (20%) - Clarity, engagement, Q&A handling
- **Impact** (15%) - Real-world applications, benefits
- **Teamwork** (15%) - Collaboration, organization, roles

**Judging Panel**:
- University faculty from engineering departments
- Industry professionals from partner companies
- Alumni working in relevant fields
- BEARS advisory board members
- Previous year award winners
